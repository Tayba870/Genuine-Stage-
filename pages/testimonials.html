Selam W.

Addis Ababa | Anxiety Support

Genuine Stage changed my life. After struggling with severe anxiety for years, I finally found a community that understood me. The coping strategies I learned here helped me complete my university degree. I'm now able to manage my anxiety and even help others going through similar challenges.

Michael T.

Dire Dawa | Depression Recovery

When I first came to Genuine Stage, I was at my lowest point. The peer support group gave me hope when I had none. Today, I'm one year into my recovery journey and feeling better than I have in a decade. The resources and community here saved my life.

Amina K.

Hawassa | Family Support

As a mother of a child with autism, I felt completely alone until I found Genuine Stage. The parent support group helped me understand my child's needs better and connect with other families facing similar challenges. The resources provided helped me advocate for my child's needs at school.

Daniel G.

Bahir Dar | PTSD Support

After serving in the military, I struggled with PTSD for years without understanding what was happening to me. Genuine Stage helped me recognize my symptoms and connect with specialized care. The trauma-informed yoga program was particularly transformative for my healing process.
