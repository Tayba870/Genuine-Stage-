What Are Online Sessions?

Our online sessions bring professional mental health support directly to you through secure video conferencing. Whether you're at home, at work, or traveling, you can access the same quality care as in-person sessions with our licensed therapists.

Online therapy offers all the benefits of traditional therapy with added convenience and accessibility. Our platform is secure, confidential, and easy to use, requiring only a stable internet connection and a private space for your session.

We offer individual therapy, couples counseling, family therapy, and specialized sessions through our online platform. All sessions are conducted by our team of experienced, licensed professionals who are trained in delivering effective virtual therapy.

How Online Sessions Work

Simple steps to get started with virtual therapy

1

Schedule Your Session

Contact us or use our online booking system to schedule your appointment at a convenient time. You'll receive confirmation and instructions via email.

2

Complete Intake Forms

Before your first session, you'll complete confidential intake forms online to help your therapist understand your needs and goals.

3

Set Up Your Space

Find a quiet, private space with good internet connection. You'll need a device with camera and microphone (computer, tablet, or smartphone).

4

Join Your Session

At your appointment time, click the secure link in your email to join your therapist in our virtual therapy room. No downloads required.

5

Begin Your Journey

Your therapist will guide you through the session just as they would in person, providing the same level of professional care and support.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about online therapy

Is online therapy as effective as in-person therapy?

Research shows that online therapy can be just as effective as in-person therapy for many mental health concerns, including depression, anxiety, and relationship issues. The key factors are the quality of the therapeutic relationship and the client's comfort with the format. Some clients actually find it easier to open up in the comfort of their own space.

What technology do I need for online sessions?

You'll need a reliable internet connection and a device with a camera and microphone (computer, tablet, or smartphone). Our platform works through your web browser - no downloads required. We recommend using Chrome, Firefox, or Safari for the best experience. Before your first session, we'll send you a link to test your connection.

How do I ensure privacy during my online sessions?

We use HIPAA-compliant, encrypted video conferencing software to ensure confidentiality. For your part, choose a private space where you won't be overheard, use headphones if possible, and make sure others in your household know not to disturb you during your session time. Avoid public spaces like cafes for your sessions.

Are online sessions available in different languages?

Yes! We have therapists who conduct sessions in Amharic, Afaan Oromoo, Tigrinya, Arabic, and English. When you schedule your session, you can specify your language preference, and we'll match you with an appropriate therapist.
