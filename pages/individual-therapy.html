What is Individual Therapy?

Individual therapy is a collaborative process between you and a licensed therapist to explore your feelings, beliefs, and behaviors, work through challenging memories, identify aspects of your life you'd like to change, better understand yourself and others, set personal goals, and work toward desired change.

Our therapists provide a safe, confidential, and non-judgmental space where you can openly discuss your concerns and challenges. We offer therapy in multiple languages to ensure cultural and linguistic accessibility.

Each session typically lasts 50-60 minutes, with frequency determined by your needs and treatment plan. We offer both in-person and online therapy options for your convenience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about individual therapy

How do I know if I need therapy?

Therapy can be beneficial for anyone facing emotional challenges, life transitions, or seeking personal growth. Common signs you might benefit include persistent sadness or anxiety, difficulty managing emotions, relationship problems, significant life changes, trauma, or simply wanting to understand yourself better.

How long does therapy typically last?

The duration varies based on individual needs and goals. Some people benefit from short-term therapy (8-12 sessions) for specific issues, while others engage in longer-term therapy for deeper work. Your therapist will discuss recommendations after your initial assessment.

Is therapy confidential?

Yes, confidentiality is a fundamental part of therapy. What you discuss in sessions stays private, with few legal exceptions (risk of harm to self or others, or abuse of vulnerable populations). Your therapist will explain confidentiality in detail during your first session.

What approaches do your therapists use?

Our therapists are trained in various evidence-based approaches including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Psychodynamic Therapy, Humanistic Therapy, Solution-Focused Therapy, and Trauma-Informed Care. Your therapist will tailor their approach to your specific needs.
