Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs

How do I choose the right program for me?

We recommend starting with a free consultation where we can assess your needs and recommend the most suitable program. You can also explore our program descriptions or contact our support team for guidance.

Are your programs covered by insurance?

Some insurance providers in Ethiopia cover mental health services. We can provide you with receipts and documentation to submit to your insurance company for potential reimbursement. Please check with your specific provider for details.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule a session?

We require 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling to avoid charges. You can easily reschedule through your client portal or by contacting our office. We understand emergencies happen and will work with you in those situations.

Do you offer sliding scale fees?

Yes, we have a limited number of sliding scale spots available based on financial need. Please contact us to discuss your situation and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
