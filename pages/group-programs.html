The power of group therapy and workshops

What Are Group Programs?

Our group programs bring together individuals facing similar challenges to share experiences, learn from each other, and develop coping strategies under the guidance of licensed therapists. These sessions create a supportive community where members can feel understood and less alone in their struggles.

We offer both therapeutic groups (focused on specific mental health concerns) and psychoeducational workshops (teaching skills for personal growth). Groups typically meet weekly for 8-12 sessions, with 6-12 participants per group to ensure everyone gets attention.

All groups are facilitated by experienced mental health professionals who create a safe, confidential space for sharing and growth. Many participants find group therapy to be equally or even more effective than individual therapy for certain concerns.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about group programs

How is group therapy different from individual therapy?

While individual therapy focuses solely on you and your therapist, group therapy involves sharing with and learning from others facing similar challenges. The group dynamic often provides unique insights and support that individual sessions can't replicate. Many people benefit from doing both simultaneously.

Will I have to share personal details with strangers?

You share only what you're comfortable with. Groups establish confidentiality agreements, and participation levels vary. Many members find that as trust builds, they naturally want to share more. The facilitator ensures a safe, respectful environment for everyone.

What if I don't connect with the group?

It's normal to need a few sessions to feel comfortable. If after 2-3 sessions you still don't feel it's the right fit, we can discuss transferring you to a different group or exploring other options. Your comfort and progress are our priorities.

Are groups available in different languages?

Yes! We currently offer groups in Amharic, Afaan Oromoo, Tigrinya, Arabic, and English. Please inquire about current availability for specific language groups when you contact us.
