What Are Our Workshops?

Our workshops are interactive, skill-building sessions designed to provide practical tools for personal growth, mental wellness, and professional development. Unlike traditional therapy groups, workshops focus on education and skill acquisition in a structured, time-limited format.

Each workshop is carefully designed by our team of mental health professionals to address specific needs and challenges. They combine evidence-based psychological principles with practical exercises that you can immediately apply to your daily life.

Workshops typically range from one-time intensive sessions (2-4 hours) to multi-week programs meeting weekly (1-2 hours per session). They're perfect for those who want to learn new skills but may not be ready for or need ongoing therapy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our workshops

How are workshops different from therapy groups?

While therapy groups focus on processing emotions and personal experiences, workshops are educational and skill-based. Workshops teach specific techniques and strategies, with less emphasis on personal sharing. They're ideal for people who want to learn new skills but may not need or want the deeper emotional work of therapy.

Do I need any prior experience to join a workshop?

No prior experience is needed for most of our workshops. We offer workshops at different levels (beginner, intermediate, advanced) and clearly indicate the recommended experience level in each workshop description. Our beginner workshops are designed to be accessible to everyone.

What if I can't attend all sessions of a multi-week workshop?

We understand that schedules can be unpredictable. While we recommend attending all sessions for the full benefit, we provide summary materials for any sessions you miss. Some workshops also offer make-up sessions or recorded content (for online workshops) when available.

Are workshops available in different languages?

Yes! We offer workshops in Amharic, Afaan Oromoo, Tigrinya, Arabic, and English. The language of instruction is clearly indicated in each workshop listing. If you don't see a workshop in your preferred language, please contact us as we may be able to arrange one.
